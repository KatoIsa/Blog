Understanding Photoshop: An Overview

Before diving in, let's clarify what Photoshop is all about. Adobe Photoshop is a powerful image editing software used by graphic designers, photographers, and artists worldwide. It allows you to manipulate images, create stunning graphics, and unleash your creativity.

What is Adobe Photoshop?

At its core, Adobe Photoshop is an image editing and manipulation software developed by Adobe Inc. It's part of the Adobe Creative Cloud suite, which includes various creative software applications for different purposes. Photoshop stands out as a versatile and industry-standard program that offers an extensive range of features for visual content creation and enhancement.
